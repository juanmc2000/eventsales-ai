EventSales AI
AI-Powered Commercial Intelligence & Response Acceleration for Hospitality Groups
________________________________________
Product Vision
EventSales AI is a commercial operating platform for hospitality event sales.
The platform helps restaurant groups:
•	respond to enquiries faster
•	optimise minimum spend pricing intelligently
•	improve occupancy
•	accelerate deposits
•	standardise sales execution
•	reduce operational overhead
•	and scale high-performing event sales processes across every venue.
The platform is designed to automate and accelerate operational workflows while allowing event sales leadership teams to define:
•	pricing strategy
•	communication standards
•	escalation rules
•	and commercial behaviour centrally.
________________________________________
Core Product Philosophy
1. Speed Wins Bookings
Most customers enquire with multiple venues simultaneously.
The venue that:
•	responds first
•	sounds most organised
•	provides pricing quickly
•	and gets to deposit fastest
usually wins the booking.
The platform must minimise the time between:
•	enquiry
•	proposal
•	and deposit payment.
________________________________________
2. AI Executes Configured Commercial Strategy
The platform is NOT:
•	drafting-assistant software
•	approval-queue software
•	or manual co-pilot tooling.
Instead:
•	leadership teams configure strategy once
•	the platform executes consistently at scale.
The system operates using:
•	configurable pricing logic
•	configurable communication personas
•	configurable escalation rules
•	configurable occupancy strategy
•	configurable follow-up behaviour.
________________________________________
3. Scale Top Performer Execution Across Every Venue
The platform’s purpose is to allow hospitality groups to scale:
•	their best pricing instincts
•	best sales behaviours
•	best communication standards
•	and best operational execution
across the entire portfolio.
Including:
•	flagship venues
•	seasonal venues
•	lower-volume venues
•	regional venues.
________________________________________
4. Revenue Optimisation > Maximum Price
The objective is NOT simply:
•	highest minimum spend.
The objective is:
•	highest expected revenue outcome.
This requires balancing:
•	occupancy
•	conversion probability
•	demand conditions
•	booking lead time
•	venue utilisation
•	and customer intent.
________________________________________
